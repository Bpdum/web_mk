About MK Squared Communications Pty Ltd

Is a Lesotho based Information and Communications Technology (ICT) Company owned 100% by Basotho professionals with more than thirty (30) years ICT experience. Their experience and expertise in this industry sets the company above the rest when it comes to end user solutions. The major business focus is data and voice solutions for small to large corporate businesses where we supply, install and support PABX systems, construct telephone networks, software and website development, supply and install data loggers, supply user electronics as well as constructing computer networks and all ICT related services. We boast in our vast experience at designing, implementing and supporting cost-effective ICT software and infrastructure solutions.
